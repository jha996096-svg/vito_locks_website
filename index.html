About Vito Locks

Your trusted partner in security hardware manufacturing.

Founded in 1965, Vito Locks is a legacy manufacturer of precision security hardware, operating our factory at 12 km Sihore, Near Mohakam Baba Temple, GT Road, Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India. With a dedicated workforce of 500+ skilled craftsmen and engineers, we serve residential, commercial, and industrial markets worldwide.

We offer full OEM and ODM services — from custom branding and packaging to unique key-way and locking mechanism development. Our in-house R&D team ensures continuous product innovation aligned with global security standards.

Our Manufacturing Process

Precision at every step — from raw material to finished product.

01
Raw Material Sourcing

We use certified brass, zinc alloy, and hardened steel from approved suppliers with full traceability.

02
CNC Machining & Die Casting

High-precision CNC lathes and die-casting machines ensure tight tolerances on all lock components.

03
Surface Treatment & Plating

Electroplating, powder coating, and polishing for corrosion resistance and aesthetic finish.

04
Assembly & Quality Control

100% key-insertion checks, cycle testing (10,000+), and salt-spray corrosion testing on every batch.

05
Packing & Export

Custom blister packs, color boxes, or bulk export packing with proper pallet and container loading.
